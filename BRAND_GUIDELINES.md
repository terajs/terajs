# Terajs™ Brand Guidelines

These guidelines describe how to reference Terajs™ in writing, documentation, marketing, and community materials.

---

## Brand Name

The correct name is:

**Terajs**  
Pronounced: *“terra‑jay‑ess”*

Always capitalize the **T**.

---

## Trademark Symbol

Use the ™ symbol in:

- first or most prominent mention  
- footers  
- legal or policy documents  

Example:

> Terajs™ is a fine‑grained UI framework.

Subsequent mentions may use “Terajs” without the symbol.

---

## Do Not Alter the Name

Do **not** stylize the name as:

- teraJS  
- TERAJS  
- TeraJS  
- Tera.js  
- TerraJS  
- TerraJS Framework  

The canonical form is **Terajs**.

## Naming Your Own Projects

You may not name your project:

- Terajs Something  
- Something Terajs  
- TerajsJS  
- Terajs Framework  
- Terajs UI  
- TeraKit  
- TeraCore  

Choose a name that is clearly distinct.

---

## Acceptable Phrases

- “Built with Terajs”  
- “Powered by Terajs”  
- “A library for Terajs”  
- “A plugin for Terajs”  

These are allowed as long as they do not imply official status.

---

## Unacceptable Phrases

- “Official Terajs plugin” (unless approved)  
- “The Terajs framework for X”  
- “The Terajs company”  
- “Terajs-certified”
